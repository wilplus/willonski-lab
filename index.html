Featured Research

The Named Emotion (NE) Framework as a Way to Measure Digital Literacy

Our groundbreaking research introduces a new framework for measuring digital literacy through emotional responses. By understanding how individuals name and process emotions while engaging with technology, we can better assess their digital competence and adaptability.

This framework provides organizations with a novel approach to evaluating employees' digital readiness and designing more effective training programs.
