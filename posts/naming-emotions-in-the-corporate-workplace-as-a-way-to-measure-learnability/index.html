The Named Emotion (NE) Framework as a Way to Measure Digital Literacy

Our groundbreaking research introduces a new framework for measuring digital literacy through emotional responses. By understanding how individuals name and process emotions while engaging with technology, we can better assess their digital competence and adaptability.

Naming Emotions in the Corporate Workplace as a Way to Measure Learnability

This study explores how the ability to identify and articulate emotions in the workplace correlates with learning agility and adaptability. Our findings suggest that employees who can accurately name their emotional states demonstrate higher learnability and better performance during organizational change.
